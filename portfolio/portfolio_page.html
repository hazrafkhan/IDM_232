East Coast Festival 2015 - Logo Design

  • 2 May 2017

  • Logo Design

On October 2015, my logo submission was selected as the winner representing the first East Coast Festival (ECFEST). The event was organized by the US East Coast Presidential Council (US-EPIC) and was attended by Malaysian students across the States. Khairy Jamaluddin Abu Bakar who is the Malaysian federal minister for Youth and Sports also attended as the VIP of the event.
